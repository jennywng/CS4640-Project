About

A quintessential problem for people around the world is finding a restroom to use in a time of need. For most people, bathrooms are not a factor of consideration when they travel somewhere. Then, when the need arises, they are stuck in a panic. Well panic no more, for Flushd helps you find the closest, best rated bathrooms to satisfy your bodily functions.
